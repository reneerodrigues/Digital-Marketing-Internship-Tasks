Task 1: Social Media Content Strategy - Vida & Vinho Café

1. Brand Overview & Target Audience

Brand Name: Vida & Vinho Café (Fictional, cute, cosy café in Goa)
Niche: Specialty Goan-grown coffee, local wood-fired bakes (Poi, Focaccia), tasty, sweet baked goods and treats, and a relaxed aesthetic workspace.
Target Audience (Instagram): Tourists, foodies, and Gen-Z/Millennial travelers looking for cute and aesthetic café experiences.
Target Audience (LinkedIn): Digital nomads, remote workers, local entrepreneurs, Instagram influencers, and hospitality partners.

2. Platform Strategy & Thought Process

Instagram: Focused heavily on rich visuals, "susegado" (laid-back) vibes, and product highlights (like our fresh Focaccia sandwiches and visually appealing desserts) to drive foot traffic.
LinkedIn: Positioned the café as a remote work hub and highlighted our hyper-local supply chain (sourcing from local Goan bakers/farmers) to build corporate goodwill and community impact.

3. Findings & Hashtag Strategy

High-intent local hashtags like #GoaCafe and #FontainhasGoa were utilized for Instagram to capture geographic searches by tourists as well as locals.
B2B and sustainability hashtags like #SustainableHospitality and #VocalForLocal were targeted on LinkedIn to engage the local business ecosystem.
