How one 20-seater cafe in Goa has embraced the idea of creating a local supply chain. 🌿☕

Since the inception of Vida Café, we have been committed to supporting the farming industry in Goa. Rather than sourcing our ingredients from elsewhere, 85% of our offerings use hyper-local ingredients. 
For instance, our cashews come from a family orchard in Savordem, our seasonal mangoes are harvested from our farm folks, Tulsidas, Sunita and Umesh gently pick and choose the best of Goa’s sun, one mango at a time.
Through our locally sourced supply chain, we not only minimize food miles but also promote tourism by ensuring that local farmers benefit from the hospitality industry.
