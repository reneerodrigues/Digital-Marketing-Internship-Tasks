1. Executive Summary
The following report presents the results of a preliminary Search Engine Optimization (SEO) analysis of padariaprazeres.com. The main aim of this SEO analysis is to ensure that the website becomes optimized for capturing high-intent visitors who are interested in food tourism in Goa.

3. Technical SEO Audit and Found Problems

A. Meta Titles and Meta Descriptions

Problem: Presently, the homepage Meta Title only says Padaria Prazeres, and the Meta Description does not include targeted keywords such as Panjim, Goa, or artisan bakery.

Consequences: Search engines do not have a clear indication of geographical location and specialization, thus leading to low search rankings among other restaurants in Goa that attract tourists.

B. Heading Tags Structure (H1, H2, H3)

Problem: There are multiple H1 heading tags used throughout the main page sections just for styling text fonts, as well as the absence of structured headings such as H2 and direct jumping from H1 to H3/H4 item descriptions.

Consequences: Disorientation of web crawlers on the structure and semantic composition of the webpage.

C. Page Speed Performance and Core Web Vitals

Problem: Low performance score on mobile devices due to large and unoptimized images of the restaurant menu and cake samples, as well as café interiors.

Consequences: Higher bounce rates among visitors with local mobile cellular networks while wandering around Panaji on foot.

4. Actionable Recommendations (1-page summary)

Optimized Meta Data: Change the Meta Title to: Padaria Prazeres Artisan Bakery and Café in Panaji, Goa. Change the Meta Description to: Savor the finest quality Pastel de Nata, fresh sourdough bread, and traditional European pastries at Padaria Prazeres in Panaji, Goa. See our Menu here.

Corrected Heading Structure: Implement a neat structure of headings. Utilize one H1 tag for your branded headline ("Padaria Prazeres Artisan Bakery and Café in Goa"). Employ H2 headings for main sections of the web page content ("Our Daily Made Pastries", "Visit Us at Panjim Bakery") and H3 headings for separate food items.

Next-Gen Image Files: Compress all HD photos of food products currently using large image files (.jpg and .png extensions). Optimize all photos by switching the image files to the new-generation .webp extension and compressing each photo to less than 150KB.

Local SEO Schema Markup: Insert LocalBusiness structured data (Schema Markup) in HTML code to indicate the physical presence of Padaria Prazeres in Caranzalem/Panaji. Specify business operating hours and contact information for optimal local search engine ranking.
