Task 4: Paid Advertising Copy - Resolution Campaign for the New Year

1. Summary

The following document is the outline of a highly conversion-oriented advertising campaign that will be executed using search and social media platforms. It consists of a target audience matrix along with copywriting meant for conversions.

2. Google Ads Search Campaign

Headline 1: Quit Joining Gyms You Never Use
Description 1: Tired of wasted gym memberships? Experience custom workout plans built for busy routines.

Headline 2: New Year, New Goals - Join Now
Description 2: Achieve your New Year resolutions with certified personal trainers and elite equipment.

Headline 3: Busy Schedule? No Problem
Description 3: Short on time? Our science-backed workflows maximize fat loss and muscle building fast.

3. Three Facebook & Meta Ad Campaigns

Objective of the Campaign: Creation of brand awareness and leads via a visual lifestyle offering campaign.
Visual Creative Offering: A highly energetic visual showcasing a young professional weight training in a neat and modern gym, with an accompanying text overlay that reads, "End of your workday. Time to Start Your Fitness."

Facebook Campaign Targeting Criteria
Location: Panaji, Goa, plus a 15 km radius around key corporate and technology zones.
Age Group: 22 - 35.
Gender: Any.
Language Preference: English.

Dedicated Targeting Interests & Behaviors
Primary Interests:
Physical fitness, weightlifting, HIIT, running, and yoga.
Behavioral Characteristics:
Frequent travelers, corporate professionals, those who like balance in their work life, and health-focused shoppers.
